We are offering targeted link popularity campaign as a crucial part of the web site promotion. The search engine optimization services are not enough to guarantee you a good position within SERP. To increase link popularity you need relevant links pointing to your web site from important sites with similar theme. Search engines see these links as votes for your web site, so the more the votes, the better the ranking. Also, having links on other web sites is a traffic source for your site.

Google has come with his own version for link popularity called Page Rank or PR. They have their own formula for calculating PR but in simple words, a page is transferring a part of its PR to the links found on that page. Beside the page rank of the site, there are a few other factors to consider like the total number of the links found on that page, the position of the link and the relevancy. While the first two are pretty common, relevancy means that the search engines give higher importance to links coming from similar web sites.
