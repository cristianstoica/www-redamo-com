The key principle to efficient internet marketing is that online businesses must have visitors and traffic to visit their websites in order to generate online sales. The more targeted and timely that traffic, the higher the probability of making a sale to those visitors.

The secret to Internet marketing success, just like with any offline business for that matter, is targeted marketing strategy. Before you get to work, you need to define the steps you will take to meet your objective. For example, strategies may aim at improving online communication and information, building awareness of and interest in your company on the Internet, and communicating its existence and advantages to existing clients. The best solution will be determined by your business model and by the goals you have set for yourself.

There are several traffic generating solutions that may help your company attract the targeted website visitors. Efficient search engine marketing through targeted keyword will direct traffic to your web site according to specific subject categories. Targeted advertising on popular high traffic web sites is a more selective tool but it is all the more likely to draw potential customers, rather than mere visitors. Pop advertising is a cost effective solution, but it is unlikely to get your business the desired attention. On the contrary, if behavioral strategists are to be trusted, most pop-ups are blocked well before they have even been fully displayed. Targeted e-mail advertising is the safest and probably the quickest way to distribute your stand-out advertisment to thousands of customers. However, it only works if the contents are highly personalized and hence requires time-consuming, careful and costly maintenance of the client databases.

Careful planning will help you make the best of all these solutions and avoid some of their intrinsic challenges. At the end of the day you will find internet marketing to be a valuable addition to your overall strategy.
